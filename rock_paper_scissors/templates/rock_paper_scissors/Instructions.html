Instructions

In this study you will play three rounds of "Rock-Paper-Scissors".

At the beginning, you will be randomly assigned to an opponent and will keep playing against that same opponent in all three rounds.

The two playes chooses either "Rock", "Paper" or "Tails".

The objective is to select an option which defeats that of the opponent. Options are resolved as follows:

  • Rock blunts, breaks, or crushes scissors: rock defeats scissors.
  • Scissors cut paper: scissors defeats paper.
  • Paper covers, sands or captures rock: paper defeats rock.

The winner alway win 100 points. If both players choose the same option, the game is tied. The game is sometimes played as best of three.

The instructions are exactly the same for all participants, and will remain visible for your convenience at the bottom of all pages.
