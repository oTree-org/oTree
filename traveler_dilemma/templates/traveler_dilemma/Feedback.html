Suppose that you claim the antiques are worth 50 points and the other traveler claims they are worth 100 points. What would you and the other traveler receive in compensation from the airline?

Solution: You would receive 52 points and the other traveler 48 points.

Explanation: You and the other traveler made different claims and yours was lower. Hence your claim, equal to 50, was paid to both of you. In addition, you received a reward of 2 and in total got 50+2=52 points. The other traveler received a penalty of 2 and in total got 50-2=48 points.
